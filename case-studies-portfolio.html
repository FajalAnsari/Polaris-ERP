Overcoming Operational Obstacles

Key Challenges

    Crystal Clear Cost Analytics

      Limited Visibility In Food Costing And Consumption Tracking.
      • Limited insight into consumption patterns and profitability analysis.
      • Inability to accurately determine the cost of ingredients and menu items.

    Effortless Inventory Mastery

      Inefficient Supply Chain And Inventory Management.
      • Difficulty in accurately tracking inventory levels and managing procurement processes.
      • Manual and time-consuming inventory process that does not provide real-time purchases and stocks that leads to wastage and increased costs.

    Seamless Cross-Department Harmony

      Lack Of Collaboration And Workflow Optimization Due To Multiple Disintegrated Systems Across Departments.
      • Multiple disintegrated systems and database across departments hindering communication and operational efficiency.
      • Lack of streamlined processes resulting in delays in reporting and data/transaction reconciliations.

    Enhanced FOH Security

      Insufficient Security Measures And Access Level Controls In The Front Of House (FOH) Operations.
      • Lack in system security measures exposes the FOH operations to potential unauthorized transactions, increasing the risk of financial loss and compromised sales reporting.
      • Insufficient access level controls make it difficult to restrict user privileges and limit access to sensitive information or critical functionalities, creating potential security gaps and increasing the chances of fraudulent activities.
