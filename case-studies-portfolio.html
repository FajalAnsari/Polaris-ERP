The Chinese Palace Restaurant Group's Journey With Polaris ERP

Chinese Palace Group is a pioneer of Asian Cuisine and operate the most famous East Asian restaurant chains in the United Arab Emirates.

Since opening its first restaurant in Dubai’s Al Ghurair Centre in 1980, Chinese Palace Restaurant has been widely acclaimed by countless customers for its authentic Chinese cuisine and attentive service.

With more than 42 years of experience in the food industry and overwhelming support from local partners and communities, Chinese Palace Restaurant has been regarded as a leading pioneer in various Asian quick and casual dining restaurants in the region.

Key Challenges

    Crystal Clear Cost Analytics

      Limited Visibility In Food Costing And Consumption Tracking.
      • Limited insight into consumption patterns and profitability analysis.
      • Inability to accurately determine the cost of ingredients and menu items.

    Effortless Inventory Mastery

      Inefficient Supply Chain And Inventory Management.
      • Difficulty in accurately tracking inventory levels and managing procurement processes.
      • Manual and time-consuming inventory process that does not provide real-time purchases and stocks that leads to wastage and increased costs.

    Seamless Cross-Department Harmony

      Lack Of Collaboration And Workflow Optimization Due To Multiple Disintegrated Systems Across Departments.
      • Multiple disintegrated systems and database across departments hindering communication and operational efficiency.
      • Lack of streamlined processes resulting in delays in reporting and data/transaction reconciliations.

    Enhanced FOH Security

      Insufficient Security Measures And Access Level Controls In The Front Of House (FOH) Operations.
      • Lack in system security measures exposes the FOH operations to potential unauthorized transactions, increasing the risk of financial loss and compromised sales reporting.
      • Insufficient access level controls make it difficult to restrict user privileges and limit access to sensitive information or critical functionalities, creating potential security gaps and increasing the chances of fraudulent activities.

A Game-Changing Solution Unleashed

Implemented Solution

Chinese Palace Restaurant Group implemented Polaris ERP on March 2020, a unified platform and restaurant industry-specific software solution designed to streamline operations, enhance efficiency, and drive profitability. Some of the key features and functionalities of Polaris ERP the were implemented include:

Recipe Management And Cost Of Goods Sold (COGS) Control

Centralized Procurement And Inventory Management System With Real-Time Inventory Tracking And Automated Procurement Processes.

Unified Platform Across All Department Including Finance, Purchase, Operations And HR Department For Enhanced Collaboration And Workflow Optimization.

Anti-Theft Prevention System

Centralized Data Management, Reporting, And Analytics For Improved Decision-Making.
